About Me

I’m a Kenyan-based voice-over artist, performing poet, and corporate MC/host with a passion for storytelling in all its forms

The Power of Voice, Poetry & Presence

I am a multi-passionate creative professional who believes in the power of voice and the rhythm of words. My journey began on stages reciting poetry that speaks truth to power—and grew into a thriving career lending my voice to commercials, documentaries, podcasts, and brands. When I’m not behind a mic, you’ll find me hosting corporate events, panel discussions, and live shows with poise and charisma—or performing original poetry that blends culture, consciousness, and emotion. I bring professionalism, authenticity, and creativity to every project I touch.
